For the students who are still with us, let's delve into the science of it all. When you practice activities or engage cognitively, you fire neurons in your brain. These neurons are fired in groups and the ones that are used often become stronger. When you next fire a neuron in the network you have created, you will stimulate the other ones as-well. The opposite is also true the one's you don't use get eliminated. Especially if it's new information.
